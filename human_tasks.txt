OCDN — HUMAN TASKS (GO-TO-MARKET)
===================================

You are the human. You attract humans. Money amplifies reach.
The tech is the substrate; the content is the product.


═══ 1. THE FOUNDING INSCRIPTION ═══

  Write a short, permanent statement: why OCDN exists.
  Not a technical spec. A declaration.
  Think: 95 Theses, Cypherpunk Manifesto, Declaration of Independence.
  Tone: conviction, not hype. This lives on Bitcoin forever.
  It becomes the first real inscription. Everything after references it.
  Inscribe it the moment mainnet is live.


═══ 2. NYC MAN-ON-THE-STREET VIDEOS ═══

  Concept: "What would you inscribe to Bitcoin — permanently, forever?"

  Format:
    - 60-90 second clips, vertical (TikTok/Reels/Shorts)
    - Approach strangers. Ask the question. Film the reaction.
    - Offer to inscribe their answer on the spot (show them the app)
    - End card: their inscription's block height + txid + the URL
    - Post-video: the inscription is verifiable. Viewer can check.

  Variations:
    - "What does the world need to know?"
    - "What's one thing you'd make impossible to delete?"
    - "If you could write one sentence on the most secure computer
      network in human history, what would it be?"
    - "What would you pay to say — forever?"

  Distribution: TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, X/Twitter.
  Hashtags: #InscribeIt #BitcoinForever #OCDN #PermanentSpeech
  Goal: each video IS content marketing AND produces real inscriptions.


═══ 3. TWITTER/X PRESENCE ═══

  Account: @ocdn or similar. Bio: "Permanent speech on Bitcoin."

  Content cadence:
    - Every new inscription gets a tweet with the embed card
      (once embeddable URLs exist — see technical_tasks.txt #3)
    - Quote-tweet news about censorship, platform bans, deplatforming
      with "This couldn't happen on Bitcoin."
    - Thread: explain OCDN in 5 tweets. Pin it.
    - Engage crypto twitter, free speech communities, journalist circles
    - Reply to every high-profile deplatforming event with
      "Inscribe it. [link]"

  Paid amplification:
    - Boost the man-on-the-street videos ($500-2k each on best performers)
    - Promote the founding inscription tweet
    - Target: Bitcoin community, free speech advocates, journalists,
      crypto-native audiences


═══ 4. SEED NOTABLE INSCRIPTIONS ═══

  Don't wait for organic users. Inscribe notable content yourself.

  Candidates:
    - Suppressed news articles (with attribution)
    - Historical whistleblower documents (public domain)
    - Censored social media posts from public figures
    - The text of the First Amendment
    - Excerpts from banned books
    - Leaked corporate memos (already public via journalism)
    - Quotes from dissidents: Solzhenitsyn, Mandela, Assange, Snowden

  Each inscription gets a tweet: "Now permanent on Bitcoin. Block N."
  The archive itself becomes the pitch. Browse it → understand the product.


═══ 5. JOURNALIST / ACTIVIST OUTREACH ═══

  Target: people who have personally experienced censorship or
  platform suppression. They understand the value proposition viscerally.

  Approach:
    - DM journalists who've had articles pulled or accounts suspended
    - Offer to inscribe their suppressed work for free (you pay the sats)
    - "Your article was deleted from [platform]. We inscribed it to
      Bitcoin. It can never be removed. Here's the proof: [link]"
    - They will tweet about it. That tweet is the best marketing you
      can buy.

  Specific communities:
    - Press freedom orgs (EFF, Freedom of the Press Foundation, RSF)
    - Crypto journalists (already sympathetic to the tech)
    - Independent journalists on Substack who've left mainstream media
    - Activists in countries with active censorship (Iran, China, Russia,
      Myanmar, Turkey — they have diaspora communities in NYC)


═══ 6. CRYPTO COMMUNITY LAUNCH ═══

  Channels:
    - Bitcoin Twitter: thread explaining the protocol, tag prominent voices
    - r/Bitcoin, r/cryptocurrency: post explaining OCDN (not spammy,
      technical, reference the vision doc)
    - Stacker News: native audience, they'll understand immediately
    - Nostr: post about it on Nostr (OCDN uses Nostr for ephemeral layer,
      natural overlap)
    - Bitcoin podcasts: pitch yourself as a guest. Story: "I built a
      permanent forum on Bitcoin. Here's why."
    - Bitcoin conferences: demo the app live, inscribe audience messages

  Paid:
    - Sponsor a Bitcoin podcast episode ($1-5k)
    - Stacker News promoted post
    - Bitcoin Magazine banner or sponsored article


═══ 7. THE "INSCRIBE IT" MEME ═══

  Create a simple, repeatable meme format:

    [screenshot of censored/deleted content]
    "Inscribe it."
    [link to OCDN]

  This works for:
    - Every time a tweet gets deleted
    - Every time a YouTube video gets pulled
    - Every time a subreddit gets banned
    - Every time a government blocks a website

  The meme IS the call to action. Two words. "Inscribe it."
  Make stickers. Put them up in NYC. Hand them out at conferences.


═══ 8. AGENT ECOSYSTEM OUTREACH ═══

  Target: AI/agent developers who need durable memory/data stores.

  Approach:
    - Publish the x402 API spec as an MCP tool (Model Context Protocol)
    - Post on AI/agent forums: "Your agent can write to Bitcoin.
      Permanent, uncensorable, verifiable memory. x402 micropayments."
    - Demo: build a simple agent that reads news, decides what's
      important, and inscribes a summary to Bitcoin via OCDN's API.
      Open-source the agent. The agent itself is marketing.
    - Pitch to agent framework communities (LangChain, CrewAI, AutoGPT,
      cognee) as an integration/plugin
    - The value prop for agents: durable state that survives any
      single provider going down. Agent writes to Bitcoin via OCDN →
      any future agent can read it from any indexer.

═══ 10. SPEND MONEY WHERE IT COMPOUNDS ═══

  High-ROI spend:
    - Video production boost on social ($2-5k on best-performing clips)
    - Free inscriptions for journalists/activists (cost: sats + rake,
      return: their tweets about it)
    - Podcast sponsorships in Bitcoin/crypto space ($1-5k each)
    - Conference attendance/demo booths ($1-3k)

  Medium-ROI:
    - Stickers/physical materials ($500)
    - Graphic design for OG images, brand assets ($1-2k)
    - Short freelance video editor for man-on-the-street cuts ($1-3k)

  Low-ROI (avoid for now):
    - Paid ads on Google/Meta (wrong audience, high CPA)
    - Influencer deals with non-crypto influencers (don't understand it)
    - PR firms (you ARE the PR — authentic > polished)

  The single highest-ROI dollar: paying sats to inscribe a journalist's
  censored article, then letting them tweet about it. Cost: ~$5 in sats.
  Return: their audience discovers OCDN organically.
